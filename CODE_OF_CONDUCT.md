# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive,
and healthy community.

## Our Standards

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its Code of
Conduct. The full text — including detailed examples of acceptable and
unacceptable behavior, enforcement responsibilities, scope, and the
enforcement guidelines — is published here:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by that document.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the project maintainers at:

**vinipd@gmail.com**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of anyone who
reports an incident.
